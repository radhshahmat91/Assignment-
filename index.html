Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.

What's included with my ticket?

Every ticket includes full access to the main conference days, all keynote and breakout sessions, and networking breaks. Pro and Team tickets add workshop access, recordings, and mentorship — see the pricing above for the full breakdown by tier.

Will sessions be recorded if I can't make every talk?

Yes. Standard passes include digital recordings for 30 days after the event, and Pro passes come with lifetime access to the full session library, so you can catch anything you miss.

Can I upgrade my ticket after registering?

Absolutely. Reach out to our team any time before the event and we'll move you up to Pro or Team and simply charge the difference.

Do you offer refunds if my plans change?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Inside that window, we're happy to transfer your pass to a colleague or apply it as credit toward DevConf 2027.

Is there a discount for bringing my team?

Yes — the Team pass covers up to 10 people with a dedicated lounge and priority support. For larger groups, contact us and we'll put together a custom package.

What's the best way to reach the organizers?

For anything not covered here — accessibility needs, press, or sponsorship — email hello@devconf2026.com and we'll get back to you within one business day.
